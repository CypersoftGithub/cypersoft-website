Privacy Policy

Cypersoft has implemented the following privacy policy because it values the privacy of its participants and visitors.

Data Gathering and Distribution

When a visitor comes to our website, we may gather both personal and non-personal data about them. To optimise the website's usability and user experience, non-personal information is gathered, such as visitor demographics. We also gather IP addresses to evaluate trends, track visitor movement, diagnose server issues, and conduct research and development. Your IP address is just used to identify you during a session and is not associated with any personal data. We also gather other data for wide aggregate use, such as click-stream data, HTTP protocol components, the URL of the requested resource, the query-string section of the URL, request date, request time, various access log fields, and navigation data.

If you decide to engage on our site as an interested party, you might be asked to provide certain personally identifiable information, such as your name, the name of your organisation, your job title, your email address, and details about your company. Additionally, you can be requested for your phone and fax numbers: This data is used to contact interested people, send newsletters, and alert them about any possibilities, properties, or services.
